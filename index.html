      How to play

      1. Read the clinical case at the top.
      2. Type your best urology diagnosis. Suggestions appear as you type.
      3. You have 6 guesses. Each wrong guess reveals one more clue from the case.
      4. Tap Skip clue if you want a new clue without spending a guess (limited to 2 skips).
      5. Get the diagnosis in as few guesses as possible.

      A new case every day. Practice mode lets you keep playing.
